## Contact Status Definitions

| Status | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| New | Not yet contacted |
| Outreach | Initial contact made |
| Engaged | Responding to communications |
| Meeting | Meeting scheduled or completed |
| Proposal | Pitch deck/proposal sent |
| Negotiation | Terms being discussed |
| Active | Partnership in progress |
| Closed | Partnership finalized |
| On Hold | Temporarily paused |
| Lost | Did not proceed |

## Activity Tracking

### Activity Types
- Email - Outbound email sent
- Call - Phone call made/received
- Meeting - In-person or virtual meeting
- Proposal - Pitch deck or proposal sent
- Follow-up - Follow-up communication
- Contract - Contract-related activity

### Activity Log Format
| Date | Contact | Company | Type | Notes | Next Action |
|------|---------|---------|------|-------|-------------|
| YYYY-MM-DD | Name | Company | Type | Summary | Next step |

## Template Notes

This CRM template is designed for tracking ScrollVerse partnership outreach to luxury brands in the following categories:
- Automotive (Rolls-Royce, Bugatti, Tesla)
- Jewelry & Watches (Jacob & Co., Tiffany & Co.)
- Fashion & Lifestyle

### Usage Instructions
1. Add contacts as they are identified
2. Update status as relationships progress
3. Log all activities for tracking
4. Schedule follow-ups based on next actions
5. Review weekly for pipeline management
