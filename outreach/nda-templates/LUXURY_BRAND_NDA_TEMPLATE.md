NOW, THEREFORE, in consideration of the mutual covenants herein, the parties agree as follows:

---

## ARTICLE 1: DEFINITIONS

**1.1 "Confidential Information"** includes:
- Product designs and specifications
- Manufacturing processes and techniques
- Customer data and purchasing patterns
- Pricing strategies and financial projections
- Marketing and launch plans
- Smart contract code and blockchain integrations
- Frequency authentication methodologies
- Partnership terms and negotiations

**1.2 "Purpose"** means evaluation and potential implementation of blockchain technology integration with luxury goods.

---

## ARTICLE 2: SCOPE OF CONFIDENTIALITY

### 2.1 Protection Standards
Given the premium nature of luxury brand partnerships, the Receiving Party shall:
- Maintain Confidential Information with the highest degree of care
- Limit access to authorized personnel only
- Implement physical and digital security measures
- Not reverse engineer any disclosed technology

### 2.2 Brand Protection
The Receiving Party specifically agrees to:
- Not use brand names, logos, or trademarks without express written permission
- Not associate the Disclosing Party's brand with any third party
- Maintain brand reputation and goodwill at all times

---

## ARTICLE 3: PERMITTED USES

### 3.1 Internal Review
Confidential Information may only be used for:
- Technical feasibility assessment
- Business case development
- Legal and compliance review
- Executive decision-making

### 3.2 No Public Disclosure
Neither party shall:
- Issue press releases without mutual written consent
- Disclose partnership discussions on social media
- Reference the other party in marketing materials

---

## ARTICLE 4: EXCLUSIONS

The following are not considered Confidential Information:
- Information in the public domain (not through breach)
- Information known prior to disclosure
- Information independently developed
- Information disclosed by authorized third parties

---

## ARTICLE 5: TERM

**5.1 Duration**: This Agreement shall remain in effect for five (5) years from the Effective Date.

**5.2 Survival**: Confidentiality obligations survive for ten (10) years after termination.

---

## ARTICLE 6: REMEDIES

### 6.1 Injunctive Relief
The parties acknowledge that breach may cause irreparable harm. The non-breaching party shall be entitled to injunctive relief without posting bond.

### 6.2 Damages
In addition to injunctive relief, the non-breaching party may seek actual damages, including lost profits and reputational harm.

---

## ARTICLE 7: GOVERNING LAW

This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of the State of New York, USA, without regard to conflict of laws principles.
