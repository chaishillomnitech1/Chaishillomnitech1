   - Log volunteer hours ‚Üí Earn DE

3. **Governance**: Vote on fund allocation priorities
4. **Exclusive Access**: Premium tiers for events and content
5. **Merchant Acceptance**: Partner businesses accept DE for goods/services

**Smart Contract Features**:
- **Lock Periods**: Flexible 3, 6, or 12-month options
- **Auto-Compounding**: Rewards reinvest automatically (optional)
- **Early Withdrawal Penalty**: 25% fee (goes to reward pool)
