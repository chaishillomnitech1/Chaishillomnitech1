## üö® Risk Mitigation

### Technical Risks
- **Smart contract vulnerability**: Third-party audit before mainnet
- **Network congestion**: Use Polygon for low fees and speed
- **Wallet security**: Multi-sig for treasury, education for users

### Partnership Risks
- **Slow partnership close rate**: Over-pipeline by 3x target
- **Partner non-performance**: Clear contracts with deliverables
- **Reputational risk from partners**: Thorough vetting process

### Marketing Risks
- **Low engagement**: Daily monitoring and quick pivots
- **Negative sentiment**: Community management and transparency
- **Influencer controversies**: Diverse portfolio, clear agreements

### Operational Risks
- **Team capacity**: Prioritize ruthlessly, hire as needed
- **Budget overruns**: Monthly finance reviews, contingency fund
- **Founder burnout**: Delegate, automate, take breaks
