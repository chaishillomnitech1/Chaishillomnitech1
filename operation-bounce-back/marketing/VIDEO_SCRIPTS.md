## ðŸ“‹ General Production Notes

### Visual Style Guidelines
- **Color Grading**: Warm tones, natural lighting
- **Camera Work**: Mix of steady shots and handheld for authenticity
- **Music**: Uplifting, hopeful, not overly dramatic
- **Pacing**: Quick for social, slower for long-form

### Voice Guidelines
- **Chef Andre**: Authentic, warm, passionate but not preachy
- **Beneficiaries**: Dignified, hopeful, grateful but not pitiful
- **Partners**: Professional but human, focus on values not marketing
- **Volunteers**: Enthusiastic, relatable, genuine

### B-Roll Needs
- Chef Andre cooking (various settings)
- Community events (wide and close-ups)
- Families being served (get releases)
- Volunteers in action
- Food prep and serving
- Happy moments (hugs, smiles, laughter)
- Technology in use (QR codes, phones, dashboards)

### Captions & Accessibility
- Always include captions
- Use large, readable fonts
- High contrast for text overlays
- Audio descriptions for visually impaired where possible
