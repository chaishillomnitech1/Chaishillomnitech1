## 3. Tiered Verification System

### 3.1 Tier 1: Basic (Email Verified)

**Transaction Limits:**
- Maximum single transaction: SGD 1,000
- Daily limit: SGD 2,000
- Monthly limit: SGD 5,000

**Requirements:**
- Valid email address
- Email verification
- Wallet address registration
- Basic personal information (name, country)

**Verification Process:**
1. User registers with email
2. Verification email sent
3. User clicks verification link
4. Basic profile creation
5. Wallet connection

**Processing Time:** Instant

**Use Cases:**
- Browsing marketplace
- Small NFT purchases
- Entry-level participation

### 3.2 Tier 2: Standard (ID Verified)

**Transaction Limits:**
- Maximum single transaction: SGD 10,000
- Daily limit: SGD 20,000
- Monthly limit: SGD 100,000

**Requirements:**
- Government-issued photo ID
- Proof of residential address
- Full name (as per ID)
- Date of birth
- Nationality
- Residential address
- Sanctions and PEP screening

**Accepted ID Documents:**
- Passport
- National Identity Card
- Driver's License
- Government-issued residence permit

**Proof of Address (not older than 3 months):**
- Utility bill (electricity, water, gas)
- Bank statement
- Government correspondence
- Tenancy agreement
- Council tax bill

**Verification Process:**
1. Upload ID document (front and back if applicable)
2. OCR extraction of ID data
3. Document authenticity checks
4. Upload proof of address
5. Automated verification
6. Manual review (if required)
7. Sanctions/PEP screening
8. Approval/rejection notification

**Processing Time:** 1-24 hours

**Use Cases:**
- Regular NFT trading
- Creator accounts
- Medium-value transactions

### 3.3 Tier 3: Enhanced (Video Verified)

**Transaction Limits:**
- Maximum single transaction: Unlimited
- Daily limit: Unlimited
- Monthly limit: Unlimited

**Requirements:**
- All Tier 2 requirements
- Video verification (liveness check)
- Source of funds documentation
- Enhanced background checks
- Ongoing monitoring

**Additional Documentation:**
- Bank statements (last 3 months)
- Proof of income/wealth
- Tax returns (if applicable)
- Business registration (for professionals)
- Additional identity verification

**Verification Process:**
1. Complete Tier 2 verification
2. Submit source of funds documentation
3. Schedule video verification session
4. Real-time liveness detection
5. Enhanced PEP/sanctions screening
6. Adverse media screening
7. Compliance officer review
8. Senior management approval (if high-risk)
9. Approval/rejection notification

**Processing Time:** 1-3 business days

**Use Cases:**
- High-value NFT transactions
- Professional collectors
- Institutional accounts
- Artists with significant sales

## 4. Identity Verification Details

### 4.1 Document Requirements

**Photo ID Standards:**
- Must be current and not expired
- Must be government-issued
- Photo must be clear and visible
- All text must be legible
- All corners and edges visible
- No alterations or tampering

**Proof of Address Standards:**
- Issued within last 3 months
- Must show full name (matching ID)
- Must show complete address
- Must be from recognized issuer
- Digital format acceptable if verified

### 4.2 Verification Technology

**Automated Checks:**
- Optical Character Recognition (OCR)
- Document authenticity verification
- Facial recognition
- Liveness detection
- Data consistency checks

**Manual Review Triggers:**
- Poor document quality
- OCR extraction failures
- Inconsistent information
- High-risk indicators
- System flagged items

### 4.3 Biometric Verification

**Liveness Detection (Tier 3):**
- Real-time video verification
- Active liveness checks
- Face matching against ID photo
- Anti-spoofing measures
- Secure session recording

**Benefits:**
- Prevents identity fraud
- Confirms physical presence
- Enhanced security
- Regulatory compliance

- Annual review of corporate status

- Business activity verification
- Transaction pattern monitoring

## 6. Special Categories

### 6.1 Politically Exposed Persons (PEPs)

**Identification:**
- Screening against PEP databases
- Self-declaration in application
- Ongoing monitoring for PEP status changes

**Enhanced Measures:**
- Senior management approval required
- Enhanced verification of source of funds
- Enhanced verification of source of wealth
- Detailed understanding of business relationship
- Ongoing enhanced monitoring
- More frequent reviews (quarterly)

**PEP Categories:**
- Heads of state or government
- Senior politicians
- Senior government officials
- Judicial or military officials
- Senior executives of state-owned enterprises
- Important political party officials

**Related Parties:**
- Immediate family members of PEPs
- Close associates of PEPs

### 6.2 High-Risk Customers

**Risk Indicators:**
- High-risk jurisdictions
- High transaction volumes
- Complex ownership structures
- Unusual business activities
- Cash-intensive businesses
- Previous sanctions or adverse findings

**Additional Requirements:**
- Enhanced due diligence
- Senior management approval
- Ongoing enhanced monitoring
- More frequent reviews
- Additional documentation

### 6.3 Non-Resident Customers

**Additional Requirements:**
- Enhanced address verification
- Apostilled documents (if from non-Hague countries)
- Translated documents (if not in English)
- Enhanced source of funds verification
- Additional background checks

## 9. Data Privacy and Protection

### 9.1 Personal Data Protection Act (PDPA) Compliance

**Data Collection:**
- Lawful and fair collection
- Clear purpose notification
- Consent obtained where required
- Minimal data collection principle

**Data Use:**
- Used only for stated purposes
- No unauthorized disclosure
- Adequate security measures
- Retention according to policy

**Customer Rights:**
- Access to personal data
- Correction of inaccurate data
- Withdrawal of consent
- Data portability
- Right to erasure (subject to legal requirements)

### 9.2 Data Security

**Security Measures:**
- Encryption at rest and in transit
- Access controls and authentication
- Regular security audits
- Incident response procedures
- Third-party vendor management
- Secure data disposal

**Data Retention:**
- Minimum 5 years after account closure
- In accordance with regulatory requirements
- Secure archival procedures
- Deletion after retention period

## 10. Sanctions and Watchlist Screening

### 10.1 Screening Scope

**Individuals:**
- Customer name screening
- Date of birth verification
- Nationality checks
- Address screening

**Entities:**
- Entity name screening
- Registration number checks
- Address screening
- Beneficial owner screening

### 10.2 Screening Lists

**Global Lists:**
- UN Security Council Consolidated List
- OFAC SDN List
- EU Consolidated List
- UK HM Treasury List
- INTERPOL Wanted Persons

**Regional Lists:**
- Singapore TSOFA List
- ASEAN Lists
- Country-specific lists

### 10.3 Match Resolution

**True Match:**
- Transaction blocked immediately
- Account frozen
- Compliance officer notified
- Regulatory report filed
- No tipping off customer

**False Positive:**
- Enhanced review conducted
- Secondary identifiers checked
- Documentation reviewed
- False positive determination documented
- Transaction cleared to proceed

## 11. Rejection and Appeals

### 11.1 Rejection Reasons

**Common Rejection Reasons:**
- Document quality insufficient
- Document expired
- Information inconsistent
- Failed verification checks
- Sanctions match
- High-risk profile
- Incomplete information

### 11.2 Appeal Process

**Customer Rights:**
- Notification of rejection with reason
- Right to appeal decision
- Right to submit additional information
- Clear appeal process

**Appeal Procedure:**
1. Customer submits appeal with additional documentation
2. Compliance team reviews appeal
3. Additional verification conducted if needed
4. Decision within 5 business days
5. Final decision communicated
6. Escalation to senior management if requested
